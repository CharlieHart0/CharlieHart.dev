Hi! I’m Charlie, a game developer and student currently in my fourth year at Newcastle University in the UK, where I am studying Computer Science (Games Engineering) at master’s level.

Since 2018, I have been developing games both in my spare time and as part of my University course, ranging from small experiments and gamejam projects to larger projects, such as my undergraduate dissertation project.

I have experience in both Unity and Unreal Engine, both in C# and C++, and several of my projects have been created without the use of an engine, in C++. My website also features other projects I’ve been working on in my spare time not directly related to game development.
